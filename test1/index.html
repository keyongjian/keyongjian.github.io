Hello There!

Dr Yongjian Ke is an Associate Professor of Project Management in the School of Built Environment at the University of Technology Sydney in Australia. He currently serves as a Department Editor for the Project Management Journal. His research spans a wide range of problems in delivering infrastructure projects. He is well recognised in the area of Public-Private Partnerships (PPPs). His work has recently focused on using social media in project management and applying the concept of Social License to Operate in infrastructure projects to improve community engagement.
