Research Summary

My research focuses on the dynamics of infrastructure delivery, with particular emphasis on project management and public-private partnerships in the Australian and Chinese contexts. I explore how large-scale projects can achieve sustainable outcomes through effective risk allocation, stakeholder engagement, and collaborative governance. To date, I have authored over 100 journal papers, with more than 7,900 citations and an h-index of 36. I was recognised among the Stanford-listed Top 2% Scientist.

My current research interests include:

  • Social Licence for Infrastructure – Developing a unique framework to define, assess, and sustain social licence in infrastructure projects
  • Social Procurement in China – Examining how procurement practices can generate social value in project delivery in China
  • AI Readiness of Construction Projects – Developing an AI readiness framework tailored to the temporary and dynamic nature of construction project settings
