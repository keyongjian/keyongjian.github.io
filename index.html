Research Summary

My research focuses on the dynamics of infrastructure delivery, with particular emphasis on project management and public-private partnerships in the Australian and Chinese contexts. I explore how large-scale projects can achieve sustainable outcomes through effective risk allocation, stakeholder engagement, and collaborative governance.

To date, I have authored over 100 journal papers, with more than 8,000 citations, an h-index of 37, and an i10-index of 80. I was recognised among the Stanford-listed Top 2% Scientist.
