Selected Publications

Welcome to the Selected Publications page. Here, I showcase a few key publications and provide accepted versions of manuscripts for download. This page aims to make my research more accessible and highlight significant contributions to the field. All journal papers from 2024 have their full texts available for download, and journal papers from other years will be provided soon. For a comprehensive list of all my publications, please refer to my full CV. It may take some time to sort out the copyright issues, but I am committed to continuously updating this page and providing as many papers as possible.
